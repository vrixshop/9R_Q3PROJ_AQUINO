What is a House Fire ?




  • A House Fire is a fire, caused by electricity or in cooking, that risks the house and its inhabitants. Though these are easy to prevent, situations where fire begins to spread may potentially cause serious injuries and burns. When handling with flammable objects, it is best advised to be careful of them especially in situations where it can cause house fire. With this, here are few of the multiple ways to prevent this to keep you and your families safe from harm.


Ways to Prevent House Fire


1. Turn off any devices that are not in use

  • Devices consume a lot of energy. If devices such as phones are fully charged, remove the plug, as it may pop once it is left too long and overheats.


2. Put flammable objects away from fire

  • It is best to be cautious about plugs if there are flammable objects nearby. This includes wood, paper, and gasoline. One of the most common scenarios where the given examples may burn is when cooking. Before turning on the stove, make sure that there are no flammables within the area that can catch fire.


3. Avoid Octopus Wiring

  • Octopus Wiring is a well-known term that describes too many wires being beside one another. Maintaining this may potentially cause the wires to burn because of too much electricity needed from the source it is plugged in. To avoid this, do not plug all wires into one extension, but separate them from one another and make use of other plugs at home.


4. Install Smoke Alarms

  • Smoke alarms are indicators that there may be a fire if not dealt with immediately. These are important as it alarms the people inside the house to evacuate to safety as soon as possible.
